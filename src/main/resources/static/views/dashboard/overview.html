Welcome to Cluster!

What is Cluster?

When ordering food online, costly delivery fees and steep minimum order amounts often discourage customers from completing their order. Cluster offers a solution to this problem through the use of group-ordering. By connecting nearby customers to form larger orders, delivery fees can be reduced significantly and minimum orders can be met easily as more people contribute to the order. While there exist applications that allow people to meet for a meal or split a check, our web application will specifically connect people with similar food interests and allow for an economical meal with the opportunity to meet new people.

How does it work?

First you should create a cluster or find a cluster to join. If you joined a cluster, your cluster leader should notify you when the order has arrived to meet at the designated pickup location. If you created the cluster, you are responsible for placing the order on HungryBoiler. You should contact your cluster members when the order has arrived so you can meet at the designated pickup location. Once the order is complete, press the complete button on the "My Cluster" page and the process is complete. Don't forget to visit the rating page to rate cluster members!

What do I do to start or join a cluster?

You can start by browsing clusters by clicking the "Clusters" tab on the side bar. If you see the restaurant you are looking for, join the cluster! Your contact information will be provided, and the cluster you've joined will now be displayed by clicking the "My Cluster" tab on the side bar.

If you don't find the restaurant you want to order from, don't worry! You can create your own cluster by clicking the "Create a Cluster" tab on the side bar. Simply fill out the form and click the "Create Cluster" button and you're set! You can view your cluster by clicking the "My Cluster" tab. Since you're the creator of the cluster, you are now the cluster leader. You can kick users with low ratings and click the "Complete" button to complete the order.
